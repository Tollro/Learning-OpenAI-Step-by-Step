My fellow Americans,

Tonight, I want to talk about the soul of America — what we believe, what we value, and where we’re headed.

One of the most important decisions I’ve made as President is nominating Judge Ketanji Brown Jackson to serve on the United States Supreme Court. She is an extraordinary legal mind, with a deep commitment to justice and fairness. Her credentials are impeccable, her character beyond reproach.

Judge Jackson will be the first Black woman ever to sit on the Supreme Court — a historic moment for our nation.

We also face challenges abroad — inflation, supply chains, and global security. But I am confident that with unity and purpose, we will overcome them.

Thank you, God bless you, and may God protect our troops.
